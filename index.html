About me

I'm a singer-acoustic guitarist based in London. Originally from Australia, I have been entertaining Londoners in a wide array of venues for over five years with his vast repertoire of lively rock, pop and indie covers. Whether its modern chart-toppers, stone cold classics, reviving a forgotten stomper or something a little unexpected, I have a bag of tricks to make the night come alive and tailored to your venue and patrons. I'm fully equipped with a high quality PA and anything else your venue may require.
